The furry living toy!



Origin: Germany
Height: 15-23 Inches
Weight: 7-18 Kg
Life Span: 13 to 14 years
Group: Utility
Origin of Name:
The German Spitz as the name suggests itself was a Spitz
variety developed in Germany. This is a
small dog that many confuse with the
Pomeranian but in general they are
much larger not only in height but in
stature as well.
