Your very own personal bouncer!



Origin: Italy
Group: Working
Height: 29-35 Inches
Weight: 40-50 kg
Life Span: 11 to 12 years
Origin of Name:
The name Cane Corso derives inspiration from the old
term that was used in rural activities such
as boar hunting and bear fighting, which
indicates the catch dog and was kept as
a bodyguard. Name origin from Italian
Cane (dog) and Corso (either meaning courtyard or guard),
also known as the Italian Mastiff
